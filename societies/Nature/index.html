A LITTLE BIT ABOUT US

Dharmaraja College Kandy ICT Society is the heartbeat of innovation, creativity, and digital excellence within the Rajan community. We are a dynamic platform where young minds transform ideas into impactful technology, blending logic with imagination to shape the future. From coding, web development, and graphic design to robotics, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies, our society empowers students to explore the limitless world of ICT. We inspire teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving through competitions, workshops, seminars, and real-world projects. Driven by passion and guided by knowledge, the ICT Society stands as a beacon of technological progress, preparing Rajans to lead confidently in the digital era.
