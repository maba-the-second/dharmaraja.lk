A LITTLE BIT ABOUT US

The Nature Society of Dharmaraja College, Kandy is a vibrant community of young minds united by a deep love for nature and a strong sense of responsibility toward the environment. Rooted in the rich natural heritage surrounding Kandy, our society inspires students to explore, protect, and celebrate the beauty of the living world. Through nature trails, conservation projects, environmental awareness programs, research activities, and hands-on fieldwork, we transform curiosity into action and passion into purpose. We believe that nature is not just something to admire, but something to understand, preserve, and defend for future generations. With every tree we protect, every species we study, and every message we spread, the Nature Society nurtures environmentally conscious leaders who strive to create a greener, healthier, and more sustainable planet.
